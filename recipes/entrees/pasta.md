## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons water (Optional)

## Method

1. Ingredients to make basic homemade pasta
2. Combine flour and salt in a medium bowl.
3. Make a well in the center and add beaten egg.
4. Mix well until a stiff dough forms, adding up to 2 tablespoons water if needed.
5. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Wrap dough and let rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
7. Roll dough by hand or with a pasta machine to desired thickness, then cut into strips of desired width and length.
