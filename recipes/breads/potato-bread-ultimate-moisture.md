## Ingredients

- 3–3½ cups flour
- 1 cup mashed plain potato
- ½ cup potato water + extra warm water to make 1¼ cups total liquid
- ¼ cup sugar
- 3 Tbsp butter
- 1½ tsp salt
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1–1½ hours, shape into loaf pan, rise 45–60 min.
2. Bake **35–40 minutes at 350°F**.
