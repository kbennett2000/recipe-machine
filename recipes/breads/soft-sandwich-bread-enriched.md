## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup warm milk
- ¼ cup sugar or honey
- 4 Tbsp butter
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1½ tsp salt
- 1 egg

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1–1½ hours until doubled, shape into loaf pan, rise 45–60 min.
2. Bake **30–35 minutes at 350°F** (golden and ~190–200°F internal).
