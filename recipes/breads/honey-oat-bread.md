## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- ¾ cup rolled oats
- ¼ cup honey
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1½ tsp salt
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1¼ cups warm milk

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1–1½ hours, shape into loaf pan, rise 45–60 min.
2. Bake **35–40 minutes at 350°F** (brush with butter after baking for soft crust).
