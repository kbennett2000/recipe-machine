## Ingredients

- ½ cup brown sugar
- 6½ cups flour
- 4 tsp yeast
- 4 tsp salt
- 3 cups water
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- Optional enrichment: 1 egg (for superior moisture and tenderness), extra flour
- Bake 60 minutes at 350°F

## Method

1. Mix, knead briefly, rise 1–1½ hours until doubled, shape, second rise 45–60 min, bake **60 minutes at 350°F** (internal temp ~200°F).
