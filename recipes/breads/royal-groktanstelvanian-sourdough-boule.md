## Ingredients

- 1 heaping cup active sourdough starter
- 1½ cups + 2 Tbsp warm water
- 4 cups bread flour (or 3 cups bread + 1 cup whole wheat)
- 2 tsp salt
- Long fermentation, high-hydration, Dutch oven bake.

## Method

1. Mix, stretch & folds over 2–3 hours, bulk rise 6–12 hours (or overnight), shape, final proof 2–4 hours (or refrigerated overnight).
2. Bake in preheated Dutch oven **20 minutes covered + 20–25 minutes uncovered at 475°F** (then reduced to 450°F if needed).
