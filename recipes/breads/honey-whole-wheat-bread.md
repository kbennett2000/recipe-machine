## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1–1½ cups bread/all-purpose flour
- 1¼ cups warm milk
- ¼ cup honey
- 3 Tbsp butter
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1½ tsp salt

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1–1½ hours (may take longer due to whole wheat), shape into loaf pan, rise 45–60 min.
2. Bake 35–40 minutes at 350°F (internal temp ~190–200°F).**
