## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1½ cups warm water
- Long room-temperature rise, bake in Dutch oven.

## Method

1. Mix into shaggy dough, cover, rise 12–18 hours at room temp.
2. Shape, rest 1–2 hours.
3. Bake in preheated Dutch oven **30 minutes covered + 15–20 minutes uncovered at 450°F**.
