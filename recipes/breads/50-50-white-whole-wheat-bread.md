## Ingredients

- 1½ cups whole wheat flour
- 1½–2 cups bread/all-purpose flour
- 1¼ cups warm water or milk
- 2 Tbsp honey or sugar
- 3 Tbsp butter or oil
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1½ tsp salt
- Optional: 1 egg

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1–1½ hours, shape into loaf pan, rise 45–60 min.
2. Bake **30–35 minutes at 350°F**.
