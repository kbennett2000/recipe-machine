## Ingredients

- 4–4½ cups flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 2 eggs + 1 yolk
- ⅓ cup oil
- ¾–1 cup warm water
- Braid and egg-wash

## Method

1. Knead, rise 1½–2 hours, braid, rise 1 hour.
2. Egg wash.
3. Bake **30–35 minutes at 350°F** until deep golden.
