## Ingredients

- 1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes, preferably San Marzano
- 3 medium garlic cloves, minced (3 tsp)
- 2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
